<br>This full-day, in-person workshop will offer hands-on demonstrations,
expert-led sessions, and practical case studies that walk through every
stage of the cell therapy manufacturing process — from cell collection
to expansion and fill &amp; finish.
<br>🔍 𝗙𝗲𝗮𝘁𝘂𝗿𝗲𝗱 𝗔𝘂𝘁𝗼𝗺𝗮𝘁𝗲𝗱 𝗣𝗹𝗮𝘁𝗳𝗼𝗿𝗺𝘀
<br>1️⃣ 𝗦𝗽𝗲𝗰𝘁𝗿𝗮 𝗢𝗽𝘁𝗶𝗮™ 𝗔𝗽𝗵𝗲𝗿𝗲𝘀𝗶𝘀 𝗦𝘆𝘀𝘁𝗲𝗺
<br>A flexible, multi-procedure platform that streamlines cell collection
and processing through continuous-flow centrifugation and optical detection
technology.
<br>2️⃣ 𝗤𝘂𝗮𝗻𝘁𝘂𝗺 𝗙𝗹𝗲𝘅™ 𝗖𝗲𝗹𝗹 𝗘𝘅𝗽𝗮𝗻𝘀𝗶𝗼𝗻 𝗦𝘆𝘀𝘁𝗲𝗺
<br>A closed, automated system that simplifies cell expansion while supporting
scalability and regulatory compliance.
<br>3️⃣ 𝗙𝗜𝗡𝗜𝗔™ 𝗙𝗶𝗹𝗹 𝗮𝗻𝗱 𝗙𝗶𝗻𝗶𝘀𝗵 𝗦𝘆𝘀𝘁𝗲𝗺
<br>Purpose-built to automate the final step in cell therapy manufacturing,
reducing product variability and contamination risk.
