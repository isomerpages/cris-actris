<p><strong>🤝&nbsp;&nbsp;Advancing Cell &amp; Gene Therapy Through Continued Partnership</strong>
<br>
<br>We are pleased to announce the renewal of our Memorandum of Understanding
(MOU) with <strong><a href="https://www.linkedin.com/company/thermo-fisher-scientific/" class="dGbsjCSdHqcHgjJJzMZoNubyIcMvnBkjcZpxQ" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_self">Thermo Fisher Scientific</a></strong>,
reaffirming our shared commitment to advancing cell and gene therapy manufacturing
and innovation in Singapore.
<br>
<br>This renewed partnership builds on our ongoing collaboration to establish
a joint laboratory that supports cell and gene therapy processes, leveraging
on Thermo Fisher Scientific’s expertise and advanced technologies as well
as ACTRIS’ infrastructure and projects to accelerate translational and
clinical outcomes.
<br>
<br>Together, we aim to continue developing skilled local talent, enhancing
process development capabilities, and fostering a robust cell and gene
therapy ecosystem in the region.
<br>
<br>We look forward to another successful phase of partnership with Thermo
Fisher Scientific — driving innovation, collaboration, and growth within
Singapore’s cell and gene therapy landscape. 🌱</p>
