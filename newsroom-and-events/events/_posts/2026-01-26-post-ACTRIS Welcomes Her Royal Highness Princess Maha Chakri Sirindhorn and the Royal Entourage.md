<p>We are honoured to host Her Royal Highness (HRH) Princess Maha Chakri
Sirindhorn and the Royal Entourage at ACTRIS.
<br>
<br>During the visit, Adj A/Prof <strong><a href="https://www.linkedin.com/in/eng-soo-yap/" class="ember-view" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank">Eng Soo Yap</a></strong>,
Chief Medical Officer, ACTRIS, shared about ACTRIS’s role as a national
facility that supports cell therapy clinical trials and clinical services
by enabling translational research and development and providing manufacturing
and commercialisation support for the healthcare, academic, and industrial
sectors.
<br>
<br>HRH Princess Maha Chakri Sirindhorn and the Royal Entourage were given
a tour of our translational laboratories and engaged in insightful discussions
about the cell and gene therapy (CGT) manufacturing landscape in Singapore
and Thailand.</p>
