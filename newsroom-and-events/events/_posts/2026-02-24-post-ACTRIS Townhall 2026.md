<p>What a way to kick off the Year of the 🐎! Two weeks ago, the ACTRIS team
gathered for our annual townhall and Lunar New Year celebration: a meaningful
time to align on our vision and rally the year ahead.
<br>
<br>Our Executive Director, A/Prof <strong><a href="https://www.linkedin.com/in/aloysius-ho-3410a722/" class="ember-view" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank">Aloysius Ho</a></strong>,
set out ACTRIS' priorities and goals for the coming years. Anchored by
a clear vision to improve patients' access to lifesaving therapies, strengthen
manufacturing and quality control capabilities, and build a robust local
and regional talent pool, ACTRIS continues to accelerate ecosystem growth.
We remain committed to working towards our vision as the national and regional
Centre of Excellence for cellular therapeutics.
<br>
<br>And of course, no Lunar New Year celebration is complete without a lohei
toss — and ours was as loud and prosperous as it should be!🥢🎊 We wrapped
up the day with a bountiful buffet lunch and plenty of good energy as we
head into 2026.</p>
